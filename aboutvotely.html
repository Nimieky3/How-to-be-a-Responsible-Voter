WELCOME TO VOTELY!

Votley is a Website made to educate you on ways you could become a Responsible Voter.

Here you will learn things like:

•The significance of voting in a Democracy
•Characteristics of a responsible voter
•Steps in making an informed decision before voting
•How to avoid election misinformation and propaganda
•Encouragement for fellow citizens to vote wisely
